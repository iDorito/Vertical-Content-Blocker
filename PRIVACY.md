Privacy Policy for Vertical Content Blocker
Effective Date: March 20, 2026

This extension is designed with a Zero-Data Collection philosophy.

No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or usage statistics.

Local Processing: All content blocking and DOM manipulation (removing Reels/Shorts) happen entirely on your local device.

No Third-Party Sharing: Since no data is collected, no data is ever shared with third parties, advertisers, or trackers.

Permissions: The scripting and host_permissions are used solely to inject the blocking script into the specified social media domains.
